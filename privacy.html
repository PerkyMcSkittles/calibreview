Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

Calibre View is a local library browser for Calibre ebook collections. Your privacy is important, and the app is designed to minimize data access and storage.

Information We Collect

Calibre View does not collect personal information, analytics, or tracking data.

The app accesses the following data only when you explicitly connect a library:

  • Files and folders within your selected Calibre library
  • Book metadata and cover images stored in that library

This access is read-only and limited to the folders you choose.

Cloud Services

Calibre View can connect to cloud storage providers such as Dropbox and Google Drive.

When connected:

  • Files are accessed only to display your library and download books on demand
  • No files are modified, uploaded, or shared
  • No data is transmitted to third-party servers beyond the selected provider

Data Storage

Any downloaded files, cover images, or databases are stored locally on your device. Temporary files may be removed automatically by the system.

Calibre View does not sync or back up your data to external services.

Third-Party Services

Calibre View does not use advertising SDKs, analytics tools, or tracking frameworks.

Cloud provider access is governed by each provider’s own privacy policy.
