Calibre View

Calibre View lets you browse and manage your Calibre ebook library on iPhone and iPad, using cloud storage or local files on your device.

Connect your existing Calibre library and explore your entire collection with fast searching, flexible sorting, and powerful filtering in a native iOS/iPadOS interface.

Key Features

  • Connect to Calibre libraries stored in Dropbox, Google Drive, or the Files app. You can also upload just your metadata.db file to browse your library without downloading covers or books.
  • Browse very large libraries smoothly — tested with libraries containing over 15,000 books.
  • Customize which columns appear in the book list, similar to Calibre’s desktop view.
  • Sort by any Calibre column, including custom columns.
  • Filter books instantly using tags, authors, series, and other metadata fields. Tap a tag or author to immediately narrow results.
  • Covers are downloaded on demand only when viewing book details, saving bandwidth and space.
  • Fast, full-library search with instant results.
  • View detailed book information and customize what appears on the book details screen.
  • Download books locally and share or open them in other reading apps in any format available in your Calibre library.
  • Support for multiple libraries with quick switching between them, even across different cloud services.
  • Optimized layouts for iPhone and iPad, including optional horizontal scrolling for wide tables.
  • Works fully offline once a library is downloaded (covers and books require connectivity).
  • Includes both light mode and dark mode.
